What is the Airbus A350?

The Airbus A350 is a modern long-distance airplane built by Airbus. It is used by airlines to fly people across the world comfortably while using less fuel.

A350-900 vs A350-1000 (A35K)

Feature A350-900 A350-1000 (A35K)
Size Smaller Larger
Passengers ~300 350+
Engines Powerful More powerful
Routes Long-distance Very busy long routes

Why is the A350 Special?

  • Uses carbon fiber to be lighter
  • Quieter engines
  • More comfortable cabin
  • Uses less fuel
